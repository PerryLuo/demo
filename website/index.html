Background

Perry is an inspiring software engineer who currently lives in Hong Kong. He was born and raised in China until the age of 8 where he moved to California - Bay Area with his family. He majored in Economics and International relation during his University years and embarked on a career in Finance post graduation. Although a career in Finance was rewarding, Perry always wanted to try something different and loved building something on his own. He realized as technology touches upon everything in our daily lives, the best way to understand it is to develop skills in the fundamentals of the coding languages.
